## Steps to Submit to Google Search Console

### 1. Verify Your Website
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your domain: `vocaldice.com`
4. Choose verification method:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest)
   - DNS verification
   - Google Analytics
   - Google Tag Manager

### 2. Submit Your Sitemap
1. In Google Search Console, go to **Sitemaps** (left sidebar)
2. Submit your sitemap URL: `https://vocaldice.com/sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"

### 3. Request Indexing
1. Go to **URL Inspection** tool
2. Enter: `https://vocaldice.com`
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Repeat for important pages:
   - `https://vocaldice.com/about-us`
   - `https://vocaldice.com/privacy-policy`
   - `https://vocaldice.com/terms-of-service`

### 4. Check Rich Results
1. Use [Google Rich Results Test](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results)
2. Enter your URL: `https://vocaldice.com`
3. Verify all schema types are detected:
   - ‚úì Organization
   - ‚úì Local Business
   - ‚úì FAQ
   - ‚úì Reviews
   - ‚úì Breadcrumbs

### 5. Monitor Performance
- Go to **Performance** tab to see impressions, clicks, CTR
- Check **Coverage** for indexing status
- Review **Enhancements** for rich result eligibility
